THE LEGEND CONTINUES

The Seagull 1963 collection is a tribute to the history of Chinese aviation. Originally designed in 1963, the 1963 was created to commemorate China's achievements in aviation and space exploration.

This Seagull customised edition takes the iconic 1963 and adds modern touches. Each watch is individually customised to your exact specifications, creating a truly unique timepiece that reflects your personal style.

Our expert craftsmen carefully assemble each watch using our in-house ST19 movement, ensuring the highest quality finish while maintaining the reliability of Chinese mechanical watchmaking.

THE SEAGULL TOUCH

Every Seagull customised watch undergoes our meticulous customisation process:

  • Movement Assembly: Each watch is carefully assembled with our in-house ST19 movement
  • Dial Selection: Choose from various dial colors and finishes
  • Case Treatment: Select your preferred case material and coating
  • Quality Control: Each component is inspected to ensure perfect finish
  • Final Assembly: The watch is assembled and tested to Seagull specifications
  • Testing: Timing accuracy and functionality are verified

The result is a unique timepiece that maintains all the quality and reliability of Seagull craftsmanship, with your distinctive aesthetic choices.
